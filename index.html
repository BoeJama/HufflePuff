Hufflepuff

The Hufflepuff House is one of the 4 houses in Hogwarts.
They value traits such as loyalty, hard work, persistence, and fairness.
They are the most inclusive and respectful house of the 4.

Spells:
Lumos : Lights up your wand

Expecto Patronum : You summon a spirit animal to fight off dementors

Stupefy : Knocks someone out

Expelliarmus : Takes the wand out of someone's hand

Avada Kedavra : Kills someone
